Aleiah Torres is currently in the Medical Science program at Brock University and is playing for coach Steve Delaney. She has been playing volleyball since she was 10 years old. Her club career started with 2 years at Etobicoke Titans Volleyball Club and eventually moved to Halton Region Volleyball Club where she achieved many accolades. Torres aspires to play on a professional team once she earns her Bachelor of Science Degree.

During her time at Hurricanes, Torres has won 5 provincal champion titles, and 3 national champion titles. She was also a member of Team Ontario in 2017 for both indoor and beach.
